Keywords and Name Abbreviations

  • E.T.: Edward Kelley, John Dee's scryer or medium, who would look into the "shew-stone" (scrying mirror) and relay what he saw and heard.
  • Mi:: Michael, referring to the Archangel Michael, who is one of the primary angelic communicators in Dee's spiritual diaries.
  • Δ:: Dee, referring to John Dee himself, who recorded the conversations and observed the scrying sessions.
  • Ur:: Uriel, referring to the Archangel Uriel, another significant angelic communicator.
  • A:: This abbreviation seems to indicate an annotation or observation made by Dee himself, often clarifying what he saw or heard, or providing context.
  • Lamina (Lamin): A thin plate or sheet, often metallic, inscribed with magical symbols or names. In this context, it's a specific magical implement.
  • Superaltar (Superaltare): A portable altar stone, used for religious or magical ceremonies.
  • Censer (Senser): A vessel for burning incense, used in religious or magical rituals to create smoke and fragrance.
  • Shew-stone / Stone: The scrying mirror or crystal ball used by Edward Kelley to receive visions and communicate with angels.
  • Tables: Refers to various magical tables or diagrams, often inscribed with letters, numbers, and symbols, revealed by the angels for specific purposes (e.g., the 49 Angels Table).
  • Septem Filiæ Bonitatis (Seven Daughters of Goodness): A group of seven spiritual entities or angels mentioned in Dee's system, with specific names like EL, Me, Ese, Iana, Akele, Azdobn, Stimcul.
  • Pele, Ese, Iana: Specific names of spiritual entities or angels.
  • Aemeth: Likely refers to the "Sigillum Dei Aemeth" (Seal of God's Truth), a complex magical diagram central to Dee's angelic magic.
  • Enoch's Tables: Refers to the Enochian Tables, a system of angelic magic and language revealed to Dee and Kelley.
