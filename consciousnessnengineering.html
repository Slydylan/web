Claim Boundary & Retired Resonance Claims

What changed

The earlier consciousness-engineering page made specific resonance and biological claims without enough mathematical exposure or experimental support. Those claims are retired from the core framework.

Allowed status

The framework may discuss consciousness, learning, and perception as metaphors or as computational architecture: observer state, consensus slices, memory commits, and feedback loops. It should not claim medical, neurological, or quantum-biological efficacy without controlled evidence.

Evidence threshold for any future biological claim

1. define the measurable variable
2. publish the exact formula
3. pre-register the predicted effect
4. compare against sham/randomized controls
5. report null results and failure cases
6. replicate independently

Current safe pivot

Use this layer for emergent programming interfaces, provenance, visualization, search, and saved artifact absorption. Treat biological resonance as out-of-scope until evidence exists.
